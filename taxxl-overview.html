About Nambri Tax XL

Nambri TaxXL, developed by Nambri Technologies, is a cutting-edge resource designed to automate the conversion of PDF tax returns into Excel spreadsheets. This innovative cloud-based solution offers a secure, efficient, and rapid method for extracting and organizing data from business tax forms. Utilizing Nambri brings numerous advantages, including the elimination of manual data entry, enhanced accuracy, reduced errors, and heightened productivity.

What Tax Forms Does Nambri Tax XL Support?

  • 1120: income statement, balance sheet (Schedule L), other deductions schedule, other current assets, other assets, other current liabilities, and other liabilities
  • 1120S: income statement, balance sheet (Schedule L), other deductions schedule, other current assets, other assets, other current liabilities, and other liabilities
  • 1065: income statement, balance sheet (Schedule L), other deductions schedule, other current assets, other assets, other current liabilities, and other liabilities, 8825 (coming soon!)
  • 1040: Schedule C, other expenses schedule

Nambri Tax XL Benefits

  • Nambri Tax XL saves valuable time & effort compared to manual data entry, which is particularly advantageous for businesses and individuals handling a substantial volume of tax returns.
  • Nambri Tax XL reduces the risk of errors and ensures consistent formatting, simplifying the analysis of tax return data.
  • Nambri Technology Solutions use Bank Level Data Security and adhere to the highest standards of security.
  • Automation enhances productivity by freeing up time and resources to concentrate on more critical tasks, such as analyzing tax return data and making informed decisions.
  • Automating the conversion process enables seamless integration with other systems or valuation models, resulting in a streamlined and highly efficient workflow.
